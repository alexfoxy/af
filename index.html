History

I began my career in 2008, at the age 19, when I founded my own design consultancy, OnStage Design. I focussed on providing services for the music industry, as I was a musician myself, and I quickly built a long list of clients mainly through SEO and word of mouth.

I soon gained an interest in coding and started offering web design as an extension to my design services. Over my early 20s I worked on a number of major projects both for clients and of my own conception including; a wordpress-esq website builder for musicians, end-to-end online retail solution for jewellery manufacturer, Online quoting system for a home renovation company and a social media aggregator for brands and individuals.

In 2014 I moved into mobile and my brother and I founded a started up for mobile app development, Studio Amplify. We primarily focused on creating music making apps that explored new ways to make music and the application of sound in the wellness space. We have since launched numerous apps and gained accolades from Apple, tech journalists and built a community of advocates.
